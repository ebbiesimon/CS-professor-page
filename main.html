About

Deborah is a young vibrant and self motivated individual who is passionate about environmental wellness, she's driven by the needs and necessities of her community and tries to breach the gap caused as a result of climate change and insecurity facing her country Nigeria, she works at university of Benin, Nigeria where she study Agricultural economics and extension services. She has volunteers with organisations such as the millennium campus network, The SDGs student hub-UNIBEN, and was once a delegate at the Edo model United Nations she advanced the SDGs goals.
